Photography

A picture tells a thousand words.

Photography

Photography is one of the best ways to show who we are and what we do, promoting the Scouting values. Here are some do's and don'ts for photography.

  • Encourage the following types of image:
    • Adventurous
    • Fun
    • Diverse
    • Youth-Shaped
    • Informal
    • Interaction
    • Passing on Skills
    • Inspiring
    • Citizenship
    • Teamwork
  • Try to avoid the following types of image:
    • Static
    • "Grip and grin"
    • Unhappy people or adults
    • Little diversity
    • Adults only
    • Uniform only
    • Marching
    • "Football Team" Style
    • Deliberately posed
    • No context
    • Images that propogate stereotypes
    • Solitary young people
    For more detail, including an image checklist, please refer to the Official Brand Guidelines, found here.
