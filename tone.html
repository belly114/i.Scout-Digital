Tone Of Voice

When posting online, it's important for people to interpret the correct message.

Tone Of Voice

Tone of voice is important on Social Media; if you get it right, you'll get good engagement and people will want to read your posts. Here are some handy tips:

  • Check your post for Typos, Spelling and Grammatical errors.
  • Make your language clear and simple
  • Use the following words as pointers:
    • Positive
    • Challenging
    • Informal
    • Surprising
    • Youth Shaped
    • Friendly
  • Use quotes from young people
  • Avoid acronyms and abbreviations (unless signing a Tweet using your initials)
  • Use informal language. Social Media is fun!
  • Include a 'Call to Action' where appropriate: "We are looking for some fun adults to join our team! Interested? Come and see what we do!"
  • Follow the style guide at scouts.org.uk/styleguide
  • Post regularly!
