Typography

The fonts that, when you look at them, you instantly think: "Thats Scouting!"

Headers

The Serif is The Scout Association's font for Titles, Headlines and Headings. For titles, use The Serif Black in Uppercase. The Serif Extra Light can be used, especially for subtitles for contrast. Ensure you have a consistent style with all work you create.

Body

For body text, use Frutiger when available. If unavailable, please use Arial.

These fonts are available to purchase online.

Questions?

Most of the content on this website is sourced from the Official Brand Guidelines and was correct as of April 2016. If in doubt please refer to Official Documents or your Media Development Manager (or equivalent).
